# BabyBook IAP Review Notes

## 通用说明

- 所有 3 个 IAP 商品均为消耗型购买（Consumable）
- 宝宝照片仅用于 AI 生成绘本，生成完成后立即从服务器删除
- App Store 截图中的宝宝照片为开发者自有素材，已获得监护人授权，仅用于功能演示
- 实体书为独立的第三方增值服务，不包含在本次 IAP 内

---

## Book001 - 《这是我》(This Is Me)

```
This In-App Purchase is a consumable purchase.

The baby photos shown in the App Store screenshots are developer-owned materials. We have obtained proper authorization from the parents/guardians, and they are used solely for feature demonstration and screenshot purposes.

Test Photo for Review:
Please use the following sample baby photo to complete the review:
https://res.cloudinary.com/hzsgsr4o/image/upload/f_auto,q_auto/babybook-review-test-photo_v5ni8c
(This photo is provided solely for App Review testing. It will not be stored or used for any commercial purpose.)

Purchase & Generation Flow:
1. Open the "This Is Me" picture book detail page.
2. Tap the orange "Customize Now" button at the bottom.
3. Upload the sample baby photo from the link above.
4. Complete the Apple In-App Purchase.
5. Wait approximately 1-5 minutes for AI to generate the personalized electronic picture book.

The printed physical book is an optional value-added service and can only be purchased separately through an external third-party website. It is not included in this In-App Purchase and is completely independent of the digital picture book generated within the app.
```
